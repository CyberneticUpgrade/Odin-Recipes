Fall for Spicy Chicken Ramen Noodle Soup

Warm up the Fall Seasons

Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup Sesame oil
  • 1/2 Chicken breast, with skin and boneless
  • 4 cloves garlic, thin-sliced
  • 2 tablespoons ginger, finely chopped
  • 2 green onions, cut into medium pieces, skewed
  • 2 tablespoons Sriracha sauce
  • 1 liter vegetable/beef/chicken broth (dealers choice)
  • 1/2 cup soy
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 cup noodles
  • 1/2 cup green onion, just the green part and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup button mushroom, sauteed with sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup soybean germ, cooked
  • 2 eggs, cooked and cut in half
  • Cilantro leaves, garnish
  • black sesame seeds, garnish
  • 3 chili peppers, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
Cooking Overview
  • Select the function to saute for 15 minutes in the Instant Pot and add the sesame oil, then seal the chicken for 5 minutes on each side, remove from the Instant Pot and add the garlic, chambray onion and the ginger, and fry for 2 minutes.
  • Return the breast to the Instant Pot and add the broth, rice vinegar, sriracha sauce and spices, and soy. Close the Instant Pot and cook on meat/stew for 6 minutes.
  • Open the Instant Pot and remove the chicken, on a cutting board cut into 1 cm thick slices. Reservation
  • Add the noodles to the borth and cook on the soup/broth function for 10 minutes.
  • Serve in a bowl, first the noodles, then two slices of chicken, the mushrooms, the soy germ, 1 piece of boiled egg and at the end, the chambray onion and spices.
  • Accompanied by your favorite booze or beverage (going for Sake here!)
